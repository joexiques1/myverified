What Is a Verified Digital Persona?

A MyVerified persona is trained only on content you approve, designed to reflect your voice, tone, and knowledge, clearly labeled as a digital representation of you, and accessible to subscribers in a controlled and transparent way.

Trained entirely on your approved content.

Every response your persona gives is grounded in content you've created and verified. Your knowledge, your voice — nothing invented or assumed.

Transparent to every subscriber who interacts.

Every conversation is clearly labeled as a digital representation. Subscribers always know who they're talking with — your authentic self, in digital form.

Why Creators Choose MyVerified

Creators choose MyVerified for full control over their persona's content, verified identity with protection from impersonation, and clear boundaries between themselves and AI usage.
